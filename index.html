Identify birds from a photo

BirdID recognizes birds using region-specific AI models that account for the season and the bird's plumage — and lets you hear its song.

Point your camera at a bird, or pick a photo from your gallery. BirdID returns the most likely species with a confidence score, a link to Wikipedia, and a recording of its call.

Features

  • Photo identificationGet the most likely species from one or several photos of the same bird.
  • Region-aware modelsDedicated AI models per biome across North America and Europe, with more regions on the way.
  • Seasonal plumageThe model considers the month, so breeding and non-breeding plumages are handled.
  • Bird songsListen to a recording of the species, streamed from Xeno-canto with full attribution.
  • Private by designYour identification history stays on your device. Photos are processed only to identify the bird and are not stored on our servers.

Coverage

BirdID currently covers North America and Europe by biome (eastern forests, deserts, boreal, Mediterranean, Atlantic and more). Additional regions are being added over time.
