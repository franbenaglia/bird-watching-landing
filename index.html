Identify birds by photo and sound

BirdID recognizes birds using region-specific AI models that account for the season and the bird's plumage — from a photo, its song, or both combined.

Point your camera at a bird or record its call — or do both, and BirdID combines the visual and acoustic evidence. You get the most likely species with a confidence score, its taxonomy, a link to Wikipedia, and a reference recording of its call.

Features

  • Photo identificationGet the most likely species from one or several photos of the same bird.
  • Sound identificationRecord a bird's song or call and identify it by ear — available for a growing set of biomes.
  • Photo + sound fusionCapture both a photo and the call of the same bird and BirdID combines both models for a more confident result.
  • Region-aware modelsDedicated AI models per biome across North America, Europe, East Asia, Oceania, Africa and Central America.
  • Seasonal plumageThe model considers the month, so breeding and non-breeding plumages are handled.
  • Taxonomy & songsSee each species' order, family and genus, and listen to a reference recording streamed from Xeno-canto with full attribution.
  • Private by designYour identification history stays on your device. Photos and audio are processed only to identify the bird and are not stored on our servers.
